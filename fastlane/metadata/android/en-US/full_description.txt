Boorusphere is a <a href="https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Imageboard#Danbooru-style_boards">Booru</a> imageboards viewer that focus on providing a simple yet intuitive user interface.

<b>Key Features</b>

* Support various booru-based imageboards
* Support playing videos and animated images (GIF, WEBM)
* Save favorites content
* Search with tag suggestion
* Download images and videos
* Block tags from search result
* Backup and restore data
* and many more ...
