<b>Boorusphere</b> is a <a href="https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Imageboard#Danbooru-style_boards">Booru</a> imageboards viewer that focus on providing a simple yet intuitive user interface.

<b>Features</b>

<ul>
  <li>Support various booru-based imageboards</li>
  <li>Support playing videos and animated images (GIF, WEBM)</li>
  <li>Save favorites content</li>
  <li>Search with tag suggestion</li>
  <li>Download images and videos</li>
  <li>Block tags from search result</li>
  <li>Backup and restore data</li>
  <li>and many more ...</li>
</ul>
